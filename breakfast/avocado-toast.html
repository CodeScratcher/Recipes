Breakfast

Avocado Toast with Poached Egg

  • Whole Grain Bread
  • Avocado
  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  1. Toast bread.
  2. Spread avocado on top.
  3. Poach an egg and place on the avocado.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
