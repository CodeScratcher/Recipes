Breakfast Omelette

  • Eggs
  • Diced Vegetables (Bell Peppers, Onions, Tomatoes)
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  1. Whisk eggs, pour into a heated, oiled pan.
  2. Add veggies, cheese, salt, and pepper.
  3. Fold and cook until set.
