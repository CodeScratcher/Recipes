Breakfast

Overnight Oats

  • Rolled Oats
  • Milk (or Yogurt)
  • Chia Seeds
  • Honey
  • Fruits (Berries, Banana Slices)
  1. Mix oats, milk, chia seeds, and honey.
  2. Refrigerate overnight.
  3. Top with fruits before serving.
